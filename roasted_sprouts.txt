Recipe for roasted sprouts (from Mark Bittman)

1) Medium pan heat olive oil to med-high

2) when hot place sliced brussel sprouts slice side down

3) warm oven to 350 deg

4) place whole peeled garlic cloves in pan (~7) before placing in oven.
Add salt and pepper to taste.

5) When skins are crispy (~30-40 min), sprouts are done!
